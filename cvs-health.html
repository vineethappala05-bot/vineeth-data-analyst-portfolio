CVS Health – Patient Care Analytics

Problem

CVS needed to predict patient readmissions, automate compliance reporting, and improve patient care through real-time monitoring.

Approach

  • Built ML models (XGBoost, Logistic Regression) predicting 30-day readmissions with 85% accuracy.
  • Designed dashboards in Power BI and Tableau for clinical KPIs and treatment effectiveness.
  • Automated compliance reporting (CMS, HEDIS) using Python and SSIS.
  • Integrated Epic FHIR APIs for live patient data and faster alerts.

Tools Used

Python (scikit-learn, XGBoost), SSIS, Snowflake, SQL Server, Tableau, Power BI

Business Impact

  • 31% reduction in hospital readmissions in 12 months.
  • Compliance score improved from 82% → 96%.
  • Reduced reporting time by 72%.
  • Critical alert times improved from 30 min → 10 min.
