7-Eleven – Retail Analytics & Forecasting

Problem

7-Eleven leadership needed to optimize inventory, improve store placement decisions, and reduce reporting delays.

Approach

  • Automated pipelines with SSIS and Power BI connectors to improve data refresh from weekly to hourly.
  • Developed 15+ interactive dashboards in Tableau & Power BI to track sales, inventory, and customer segmentation.
  • Used Python + GIS data to analyze store performance and new store locations.
  • Built demand forecasting models in Python & SAS to optimize stock levels.

Tools Used

SQL Server, SSIS, Tableau, Power BI, Python, SAS

Business Impact

  • 70% reduction in manual reporting effort.
  • 25% stockout reduction and 15% lower excess inventory.
  • 12% ROI increase for new store placements.
  • Boosted loyalty program retention by 18% with churn analysis.
